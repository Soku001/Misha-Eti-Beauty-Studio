Our Signature Treatment

Signature
BIAB Nails

Builder in a Bottle (BIAB) is a revolutionary nail system that strengthens your natural nails while providing long-lasting colour and durability. Unlike traditional gels, BIAB actually improves the health of your nails over time — the perfect choice for those who want beautiful nails without compromise.

Strengthens and repairs natural nails
Long-lasting finish — up to 4 weeks
Beautiful, high-gloss professional look
Safe removal that protects nail integrity
Available in hundreds of stunning colours
